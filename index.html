Generation of Three Cars

The First Generation — Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang, introduced in 1964, became an icon of American muscle cars. Its combination of performance, style, and affordability made it one of the most beloved cars in history.

The Second Generation — Tesla Model S

The Tesla Model S, launched in 2012, revolutionized the car industry by making electric vehicles powerful, luxurious, and desirable. It marked the start of the electric revolution.

The Third Generation — Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a timeless masterpiece. Combining engineering excellence with an unmistakable design, it continues to set standards for sports cars even after decades of evolution.
